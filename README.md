# Academic Theme for [Hugo](https://github.com/gohugoio/hugo)

The Hugo **Academic Resumé Theme** empowers you to create your job-winning online resumé and showcase your academic publications.

[Check out the latest demo](https://academic-demo.netlify.app) of what you'll get in less than 10 minutes, or [view the showcase](https://wowchemy.com/user-stories/).
